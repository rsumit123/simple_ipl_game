Simple IPL predictor game



  • If you have not registered , Click on Add Player
  • If you have registered , Click on Make Predictions
  • Submit Predictions to save predictions
  • After submitting predictions , you can click on match leaderboard to view your position
  • You can go to View Predictions to view your predictions
  • You can go to Points breakdown from the View Predictions Page
  • Final Leaderboard is updated after every match contains your position after summing up points from every match
